Our Treats

The Sweet Life Truck has some of your favorite treats to satisfy your sweet tooth. Prepare your tastebuds to savor items including ice cream cones, italian ice, and custom treats original to Gabby's. We have something for everyone! Want something you’ve never tried before? Grab a Cereal bowl. Want something Sweet and Light? Grab a slice of Cheesecake or Apple Pie. Want a Sweet Drink? Grab a Snapple or a Starbucks Frappuccino Coffee Drink.

We'll Be There

Gabby’s Sweet Treats makes a conscious effort to support children of all ages who participate in extracurricular activities. Invite the Sweet Life Truck to your non-profit events, and Gabby’s Sweet Treats will donate 20% of the proceeds to your organization. We are vested in the community and are serious about doing our part to support those who support us! Contact us about fundraisers, school fairs, camps, sporting events, and other community events.

About

Founded in 2019, Gabby's Sweet Treats is the brainchild of a father-daughter team, Ollie and Gabby. As a passionate athlete, Gabby is often at sporting events as a participant and spectator. She began to notice that there weren't any snacks and refreshments available. This was disappointing to Gabby- she believes that having a treat at a track meet or football game on a hot summer day makes the experience that much better. Gabby knew that there was a need for it, and Gabby's Sweet Treats was created!

We pride ourselves on enhancing sporting events with the presence of amazing dessert. “The SweetLifeTruck” visits are also the perfect addition for parties, fairs, and PTA sponsored events.

Ollie and Gabby are dedicated to bringing the fun and innovation to creative desserts to reach children and families in the DMV area. Let The SweetLifeTruck provide a special visit to your next event. We have a flavor that you will like so if you see us ask for a free sample!
